## Recommendations

Based on the audit findings, Botium Toys needs to immediately prioritize the following controls:

1. **Least Privilege & Separation of Duties** — limit employee access to sensitive data
2. **Encryption** — protect all credit card and PII data
3. **Disaster Recovery Plans & Backups** — ensure business continuity
4. **IDS** — monitor and detect anomalous network activity
5. **Strong Password Policy & Password Management System** — reduce account compromise risk

These actions will significantly reduce the risk score and help Botium Toys achieve compliance with PCI DSS, GDPR, and SOC standards.
